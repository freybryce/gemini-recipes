Roasted Beet & Persimmon Salad with Goat Cheese

This salad balances the earthy sweetness of the beets with the honey-like flavor of the persimmons. The peppery arugula, tangy goat cheese, and a bright maple-dijon vinaigrette bring all the fall flavors together.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium beets (about 1.5 lbs), red or golden
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 firm, ripe Fuyu persimmons (the squat, tomato-shaped kind)
  • 5 oz baby arugula (about 5 packed cups)
  • 4 oz goat cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup toasted walnuts or pecans, roughly chopped
  • 1/3 cup pomegranate arils (seeds)
  • 1/4 small red onion, very thinly sliced (optional)
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp real maple syrup
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Roast the Beets: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Trim the leaves and root ends from the beets. Scrub them well under running water. Place the beets on a piece of aluminum foil. Drizzle with 1 tbsp of olive oil and sprinkle with a pinch of salt. Wrap the beets tightly in the foil to create a sealed packet. Place the foil packet directly on the middle oven rack. Roast for 45-60 minutes, or until a sharp knife can be easily inserted into the center of the largest beet. Once cooked, open the foil packet carefully to let the steam escape. Set the beets aside until they are cool enough to handle. The skins should rub off easily with a paper towel or your fingers. Once peeled, slice the beets into 1/4-inch rounds or cut them into bite-sized wedges.
  2. Prepare the Vinaigrette: In a small bowl or a lidded jar, combine all the vinaigrette ingredients: olive oil, apple cider vinegar, maple syrup, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Whisk or shake vigorously until the dressing is smooth and fully combined (emulsified).
  3. Prepare the Persimmons: Trim the leafy top (calyx) from each persimmon. Slice the persimmons into thin rounds, and then cut the rounds into quarters or halves, depending on your preference. Fuyu persimmons are eaten firm and do not need to be peeled.
  4. Assemble the Salad: In a large salad bowl, add the baby arugula. You can either toss the arugula with about half of the vinaigrette or serve it on the side. Arrange the sliced roasted beets and persimmons over the arugula. Sprinkle the crumbled goat cheese, toasted nuts, pomegranate arils, and optional thinly sliced red onion evenly over the top. Drizzle with the remaining vinaigrette and serve immediately.

Additional Information

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 60 minutes
  • Total Time: 80 minutes
  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Category: Salad
  • Cuisine: American

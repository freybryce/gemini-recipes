Tangy Lemon-Herb Yogurt Dip for Latkes

A refreshing and zesty yogurt-based dip with fresh dill and chives, perfect for complementing the savory flavors of Cauliflower & Potato Latkes. It's quick to prepare and adds a bright, creamy element to your dish.

Prep time: 5 minutes

Cook time: 0 minutes

Total time: 5 minutes

Yield: Approximately 1.5 cups (serves 4-6)

Category: Sauce/Dip

Cuisine: Mediterranean, Fusion

Suitable For: Vegetarian, Gluten-Free

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (240g) plain Greek yogurt (full-fat or 2%)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice (about half a lemon)
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder (or 1 small clove garlic, minced)
  • ¼ teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • ⅛ teaspoon black pepper (or to taste)
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil, for drizzling

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, finely chopped fresh dill, finely chopped fresh chives, fresh lemon juice, garlic powder (or minced garlic), salt, and black pepper.

  2. Stir until all ingredients are well combined and the dip is smooth and creamy.

  3. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. You might want to add a little more salt, pepper, or lemon juice depending on your personal preference and the tartness of your yogurt.

  4. For best flavor, cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 15-30 minutes before serving. This allows the flavors to meld and deepen.

  5. Drizzle with a teaspoon of extra virgin olive oil just before serving, if desired, for an added touch of richness. Serve alongside warm Cauliflower & Potato Latkes.

Melted Brie & Apple Sandwich

Warm, golden-grilled gluten-free sourdough with gooey brie, sweet apple, honey, crunchy walnuts, and peppery rocket. Elegant enough for a date night, easy enough for lunch.

Prep time: 5 minutes

Cook time: 12 minutes

Total time: 17 minutes

Yield: 2 servings

Category: Sandwich

Cuisine: European

Keywords: gluten-free, vegetarian, brie, apple, grilled sandwich, GF sourdough

Ingredients

  • 4 thick slices gluten-free sourdough
  • 120g brie, rind on, sliced 5mm thick
  • 1 crisp sweet apple (e.g. Fuji or Honeycrisp), cored and thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 3 tbsp walnuts, roughly chopped
  • 30g rocket (arugula)
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tbsp whole grain Dijon mustard
  • 1 pinch freshly cracked black pepper

Instructions

  • 1. Toast the walnuts: In a dry skillet over medium heat, toast the walnuts for 3–4 minutes, stirring often, until fragrant and lightly golden. Set aside.
  • 2. Butter the bread: Spread butter evenly on one side of each slice of gluten-free sourdough. These buttered sides will face outward and go against the pan for a golden crust.
  • 3. Build the sandwiches: Flip 2 slices butter-side down and spread whole grain Dijon mustard on the inner face. Layer on the brie, then the apple slices. Scatter over the toasted walnuts and drizzle with honey. Season with black pepper. Top with the remaining bread slices, butter-side up.
  • 4. Grill until golden: Heat a skillet or griddle pan over medium-low heat. Place the sandwiches in the pan and press down gently with a spatula. Cook until the underside is deep golden, about 4–5 minutes.
  • 5. Flip and melt: Carefully flip and cook the other side for another 3–4 minutes, until golden and the brie is visibly melting at the edges. Don't rush with high heat — low and slow gives you even colour without burning the gluten-free bread.
  • 6. Add rocket and serve: Remove from the pan and immediately open each sandwich to tuck in a generous handful of rocket — the residual heat will just barely wilt it. Close, slice diagonally, and serve right away.
